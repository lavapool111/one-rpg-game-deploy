# CODA: Controls & How to Play

## Movement
- **W, A, S, D**: Move Character
- **Mouse**: Look Around
- **Space**: Jump
- **Q**: Sprint

## Combat
- **Left Click**: Basic Attack
- **1, 2**: Use Ability

## Interface
- **Z**: Open Inventory (Equip gear, manage materials)
- **E**: Player Stats
- **Esc**: Pause Game
- **R**: Close all menus

## Core Mechanics
- **Combat**: Different instruments offer unique ranges and attack speeds. Your instrument is your weapon.
- **Progression**: Defeat enemies, level up, and collect materials (reeds, valves, oils) to enhance your gear.
- **Endless Expanse**: Explore the Band Room and the deeper Backstage Halls in an endless cycle of growth and survival.
